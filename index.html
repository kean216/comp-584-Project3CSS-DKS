Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System. It carries the name of the Roman god of war and is often referred to as the "Red Planet" due to the effect of the iron oxide prevalent on its surface.

Moons: 2 (Phobos, Deimos)
Gravity: 3.721 m/s²
